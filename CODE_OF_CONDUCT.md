# Code of Conduct

This project uses a simple contribution standard: be respectful, specific, and constructive.

Expected behavior:

- Discuss ideas and trade-offs in good faith.
- Keep bug reports and reviews focused on reproducible behavior.
- Respect that memory dumps and diagnostic data may be sensitive.
- Help make the project usable for people who are not already familiar with its internals.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, discriminatory language, personal attacks, and publishing private diagnostic data without permission.

Report concerns to the project maintainers through a private channel. Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, or block participants who make collaboration unsafe or unproductive.
